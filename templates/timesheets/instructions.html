Go to the Timesheets Page

Click on the "Timesheets" link in the left navigation menu to access your current timesheet.

This will take you to the "My Timesheet" page where you can see your time entries for the current pay period.

Edit Your Timesheet

On the My Timesheet page, look for the large blue "Edit Timesheet" button at the top of the page.

Click this button to go to the timesheet editing page where you can enter your hours for each day.

Enter Your Hours

On the Edit Timesheet page, enter the number of hours worked for each day of the pay period.

You can add a description of the work performed in the description field.

When finished, click the large blue "Save Changes" button at the bottom of the form.

Submit For Approval

After saving changes, you'll be returned to the timesheet view.

Look for the large green "Submit for Approval" button at the top of the page.

Click this button to submit your timesheet for manager approval.

Important: Once submitted, you cannot edit your timesheet until it's approved or rejected by your manager.
